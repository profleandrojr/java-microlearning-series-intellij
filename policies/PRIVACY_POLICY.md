# Privacy Policy

This project does not collect, store, or process personal data directly.

All interactions occur through third-party platforms such as:
- GitHub
- JetBrains Marketplace
- JetBrains IDEs
- Google Classroom (optional)

Each platform operates under its own privacy policy and data handling practices.

Participation in external platforms is optional and not required to access core educational content.
